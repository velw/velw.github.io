Dandapani

Dandapani is a Hindu priest and former monk of 10 years who is now an international speaker and entrepreneur. He has also established Siva Ashram in Costa Rica.

Lineage

Dandapani spent 10 years at Kauai Hindu Monastery in Hawaii where he studied under the guidance of Satguru Sivaya Subramuniyaswami, the 162nd head of the Kailasa Parampara lineage of Nandinatha Sampradaya.

About the Kailasa Parampara

The Kailasa Parampara practices a monistic (advaita) form of the Shaiva Siddhanta branch of Shaivism, which it calls Advaita Saiva Siddhanta. Shaiva Siddhanta is a sub-school of Vedanta, one of the six orthodox schools of Hindu philosophy.
