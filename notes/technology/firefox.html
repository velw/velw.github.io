Firefox add-ons

  • User interface & health
    • Fixed zoom — Remember zoom settings for each site (so you don't need to lean forward into a dangerous posture to read the screen)
    • Dark Reader — Dark mode for every web site, can be configured to turn on and off automatically with sunset and sunrise
    • Unhook — Hides related video recommendations on YouTube (eliminate time and attention theft)
    • eyeCare — Periodic reminders to look away from the screen and do eye exercises
    • Reading ruler — Highlight one line of text at a time (aids with focus)
  • Research & information management
    • Raindrop.io — URL clipper
    • Buffer — Post to social media without logging into social media (eliminate time and attention theft)
    • Unofficial Hypothesis extension — Collect and organize research notes from any web page
    • Notebook web clipper — Clip web content to your Zoho Notebook
  • Language tools
    • 10ten Reader — Pops up Japanese-English dictionary for text beneath mouse pointer
  • Utilities
    • uBlock Origin — Block ads, trackers and malware
    • Ruffle.rs — Restores Adobe Flash
    • Tampermonkey — Allows custom scripting to adjust web content on the fly
