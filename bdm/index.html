Black Desert Mobile

Objectives
  • Become stronger and more capable to increase your resources and achieve your goals (like real life, only with less work, less complexity and much more encouragement!)
  • Defeat foes, solve mysteries and engage in player combat
  • Do this by developing your character's skills, knowledge, attributes and resources, by:
    • defeating monsters to raise your ??? (combat power?) and obtain items and silver
    • completing quests and ???-ing gear to enhance your black spirit, which augments your combat abilities
    • improving your combat abilities by using books to develop them and augmenting them with stones
    • selecting the best combat abilities from those available for use in combat
    • gathering resources from the world
    • developing your camp
    • improving the level and skills of workers in your camp
    • breeding pets and horses

Daily tasks

  • Daily
    • Camp
      • Acknowledge completion of tasks by camp workers
      • Collect stones from odd furnace in camp, and daily silver from camp office
      • Check new messages and collect any items
      • Move heavy items into storage (black clustery things, > 100 potions) from bag (note: sell armor and weapons on AH, below)
    • Town
      • Check auction house for sales and list as many (heavy) items for sale as possible
      • Sell junk drop items to vendor, buy pet food and hi potions
      • Apply any new skill books
      • Feed pets
      • Check available black spirit quests for xp (kill quota quests) are active
  • Before boss fights
    • Level up (use map - find area roughly x levels below - 3-4 for valkyrie

Combat

There are two modes of combat; manual and automatic.

Pets

Pets gain experience when you feed them (which you can only do when they are hungry). The more they are "out", the more hungry they get.
